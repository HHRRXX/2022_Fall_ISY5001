Book Ranking and Category Detection

Instruction: You can upload a cover image of the book through the upload button or directly drag the image into this page to get the popularity and rough category prediction. You can type the book name(description) into the text box to receive the book category prediction.

Most importantly, you can do either or both.


The output of the ranking prediction function is a tier number from 1-5 which indicates the predicted popularity of the input book cover. The rage has been divided into 5 tiers, the smaller the number is, the more popular the model thinks the book will be.
